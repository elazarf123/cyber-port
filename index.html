// Case Study · Offboarding Pipeline
Problem: Manual offboarding took ~45 min per user across AD, Exchange, Teams, OneDrive, and license reclaim — error-prone and inconsistent.
Solution: Single-invocation script: disables AD account, converts mailbox to shared, wipes Teams/OneDrive ownership, reclaims E3 license, archives data to retention container, emails HR & manager with summary.
Result: 45 min → 90 sec per offboarding · zero orphaned licenses · full audit trail.
